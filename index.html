Welcome to My Research Website

I am ENRIQUÉ DEBRABANT, a PhD student at the Denis Poisson Institute, University of Tours, specializing in Epidemiology and Mathematical Modeling of Infectious Systems. My work focuses on developing multi-strain SIS models with mutation to better understand disease spread and the evolution of infectious agents.

I am conducting my doctoral research under the supervision of PIERRE GABRIEL and Sten Madec.

Research Domain

My doctoral project combines dynamic systems theory with real-world epidemiological data analysis. The goal is to create robust predictive models to simulate different disease control and prevention strategies.

Main topics:

  • Multi-strain epidemiological modeling
  • Impact of mutations on epidemic dynamics
  • Evaluation of control measures (vaccination, social interventions)

Skills

  • Mathematical and statistical modeling (SIS, SIR, etc.)
  • Data analysis and scientific programming (Python, Sagemath)
  • Numerical simulations and multi-agent modeling
  • Scientific writing and results communication
