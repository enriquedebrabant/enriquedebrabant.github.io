Theses and Dissertations

  • Multi-strain SIS Model with Mutation (2025)

    Denis Poisson Institute, University of Tours
    Supervisors: Pierre Gabriel, Sten Madec

    This dissertation extends the classic SIS model to incorporate phenotypic diversity and mutations in pathogens, using a continuous variable for phenotypes. It provides a rigorous theoretical analysis, proving existence and uniqueness of solutions, and develops numerical simulations to study strain competition and epidemic dynamics.
